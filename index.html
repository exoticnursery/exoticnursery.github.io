Our understanding of transplanting palms and trees from local farms and other parts of the world is well documented. With over 30 years of experience, we have provided palms and trees for diverse locals.

With farms in Argentina, Jamaica, Dominican Republic, Costa Rica, California and Florida, we are in a unique position to provide a greater variety of plants with a totally different style.

When looking for large specimen palms and trees let us find them for you. Having worked in South Florida we are able to locate, prepare and transplant large specimens for that unique look.
